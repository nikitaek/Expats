### QR Code Management System

This is an important technical component. The business will likely require a large amount of work with QR codes, so a dedicated internal system will be needed.

What the system should include:

- QR code design
- Link encryption
- Links routed through our own service so they always redirect through our system
- Storage of what each link is and where it came from
- Analytics for every link visit
- Pixels from different services stored on the site for retargeting, including cases where the user does not click the main action
- Tracking of action clicks on the page itself
- Ideally, deeper attribution to know which user came from which source
- Grouping of links by category such as restaurants, taxis, signs, elevator ads, and airport codes
- The ability to assign each link to a user and provide that user with statistics for actions generated by that link

### AI for Finding and Onboarding Providers

#### How it works

AI finds businesses such as:

- Restaurants
- Spas
- Massage businesses
- Excursions
- Guides
- Motorbike rental
- Taxis
- Laundries
- Tailors

AI automatically calls them.

It performs an initial screening:

- Whether there is Russian-speaking staff
- Whether they are ready to work with tourists
- Whether they are ready to pay a commission
- Which services they provide
- Prices
- Opening hours

AI fills the CRM.

If the provider is suitable:

- A meeting is scheduled
- Or a person checks the provider in person
- Or a test order is launched
