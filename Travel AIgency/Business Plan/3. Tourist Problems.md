# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 3. Tourist Problems

### Main problem categories

- Language barrier
  - Tourists do not know English
  - Tourists do not know Vietnamese
- Information chaos
  - There are hundreds of conflicting recommendations
  - Tourists do not know whom to trust
- Lack of local knowledge
  - Tourists do not know which excursions exist
  - Tourists do not know where to go
  - Tourists do not know how things work locally
  - Tourists do not know where to exchange money
  - Tourists do not know where to find food
  - Tourists do not know how to find a taxi
  - Tourists do not know that they may be able to rent a bike without a driving license
  - Tourists do not know how to find a restaurant with a Russian menu
- Fear of being deceived
  - Taxi
  - Currency exchange
  - Motorbike rental
  - Excursions
- Payment difficulties
  - Russian cards
  - Cash
  - Crypto
- Lack of local help
  - They need a person "who knows"
