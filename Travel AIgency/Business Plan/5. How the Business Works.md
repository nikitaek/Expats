# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 5. How the Business Works

Travel AIgency does not operate through only one linear flow. It works through several operating paths depending on the tourist's request.

### Operating chart

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A["Tourist writes to us in a messenger"] --> B["AI identifies the need"]
    B --> C["AI uses CRM and context to understand location, request type, and customer details"]

    C --> D["Information and recommendation flow"]
    C --> E["Service coordination flow"]
    C --> F["Payment assistance flow"]
    C --> G["Future direct service flow"]

    D --> D1["Tourist receives guidance, options, and recommendations"]

    E --> E1["AI selects the right subcontractor or partner"]
    E1 --> E2["AI forwards or manages communication with the provider"]
    E2 --> E3["Provider delivers the service"]
    E3 --> E4["Travel AIgency receives a commission or revenue share"]

    F --> F1["Tourist has a payment problem"]
    F1 --> F2["Tourist messages us"]
    F2 --> F3["We coordinate the payment with the partner side"]
    F3 --> F4["Travel AIgency receives a commission or service fee"]

    G --> G1["For selected high-margin categories, Travel AIgency may later deliver the service directly"]
    G1 --> G2["Example: bike rental in high-demand locations"]
```

### Key logic

- The tourist communicates with Travel AIgency, not directly with multiple local providers
- The AI sits between the tourist and the partner network and manages two-sided communication
- The system routes requests based on customer context, location, and service type
- Different requests can lead to recommendation, coordination, payment support, or future direct fulfillment
