# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 9. Marketing

This is a very important section. All marketing ideas should be collected here.

### 9.1 PPC and Online Advertising

Channels:

- Google Search
- YouTube Ads
- Facebook Ads
- Instagram Ads
- WhatsApp click-to-message
- Google Maps / Google Business Profile
- Telegram Ads / advertising in Russian-language Telegram channels
- VK Ads
- Odnoklassniki / OK Ads
- Yandex Direct
- Yandex Maps
- Yandex Travel / travel audiences
- 2GIS, if relevant for Vietnam
- Aviasales / travel media placements
- Tripster / travel content and excursion audience
- Ostrovok / similar Russian travel platforms
- Rutube / VK Video for video advertising

For the Russian audience, it is especially important not to rely only on Meta and Google. Russian users still have strong habits around VK, OK, Yandex, Telegram, travel services such as Aviasales, Ostrovok, and Tripster, and map products such as Google Maps, Yandex Maps, and Maps.me while traveling.

It is also important for 2025-2026 to account for the fact that WhatsApp and Telegram have faced restrictions in Russia, while MAX is being promoted as a state-backed alternative. The advertising and communication infrastructure should therefore not depend on only one messenger.

### 9.2 Billboards in Tourist Areas

Place billboards in places frequently visited by Russian tourists:

- Near hotels
- Near beaches
- On tourist streets
- Near popular restaurants
- Near bus and transfer boarding points
- Near currency exchange points, SIM card points, and travel agencies

### 9.3 Flyers and QR Codes in Hotels

Place flyers, stands, and QR codes in hotels where Russian tourists stay.

Format examples:

- "Russian assistant in Hoi An"
- "Taxi, restaurants, pharmacies, excursions, exchange, SIM card - everything in Russian"
- QR code to Telegram, WhatsApp, or the website
- A separate QR code for each hotel for attribution

### 9.4 Advertising in Transfer Buses

Find companies and people who meet Russian tourists on arrival and bring them to hotels.

Possible formats:

- Sticker with QR code inside the bus
- Flyer on the seat
- Driver or guide says a short phrase
- QR code on a screen or sign
- Commission for every referred tourist

### 9.5 Advertising Stands in Restaurants Near Hotels

Identify which restaurants are near the hotels where Russian tourists stay and negotiate placement of advertising stands.

Important mechanics:

- Give each restaurant a unique QR code
- Track the number of visits
- Pay per lead, per order, or as a fixed placement fee
- Explain to the restaurant: "We will bring you Russian tourists, and you help us with placement"

### 9.6 Encourage Sharing

Motivate tourists to share the service.

Mechanics:

- "Invite a friend and get a discount on a transfer, excursion, or spa"
- "Share in the hotel chat and get a bonus"
- "Send the link to a friend and get a curated list of the best places"
- Referral link or promo code
- Mini-game: "Get 3 useful guides for 3 invitations"

### 9.7 Russian Tour Operators

Find Russian tour operators that sell tours to Vietnam, Da Nang, and Hoi An, and arrange for them to recommend the service to tourists before departure.

Format:

- PDF guide: "What to know before your trip to Hoi An"
- Partner commission
- White-label assistant for their clients
- Separate QR code for each tour operator
- Tourist support after arrival

### 9.8 Advertising Through Airport Transfers

Identify who transports tourists from Da Nang Airport to Hoi An and Da Nang.

This is similar to advertising in transfer buses, but with a focus on the first contact point after arrival.

Formats:

- Advertising inside the car
- QR code on the back of the seat
- Card: "Need help in Russian?"
- Commission for the driver
- Separate landing page: "Have you just arrived in Vietnam?"

### 9.9 Person in the Airport with an Advertising Sign

Place a person in the airport with a sign, banner, or branded T-shirt.

Idea:

- "Russian assistant in Vietnam"
- "Taxi / SIM / exchange / excursions / restaurants"
- QR code on the sign
- The person can distribute flyers
- This can be tested only during arrival hours of flights from Russia and CIS countries

### 9.10 Free SIM Card or Welcome Bonus

Create a lead magnet in the airport:

- Free SIM card
- Help with eSIM
- Free mini-guide
- Discount on the first transfer
- "First question free"
- Map of Hoi An in Russian
- List of verified restaurants, pharmacies, and exchange points

The goal is to get the tourist's contact immediately after arrival.

### 9.11 Taxi Driver Partnership Program

Reach agreements with taxi drivers and give each of them a personal QR code.

Mechanics:

- The taxi driver gives the QR code to the tourist
- The tourist writes to the service
- The taxi driver receives a commission
- We show analytics: how many visits, how many orders, and how much money was accrued
- A simple partner page or application can be created

### 9.12 Taxi Driver QR Code Positioning: "We Manage Trips"

Give taxi drivers a QR code, but position the service not just as advertising, but as a "Russian trip manager."

Example:

- The tourist wants a transfer, excursion, or trip
- The tourist writes to us in Russian
- We organize the trip
- If the lead came from a specific taxi driver, we first offer the order to that driver
- The taxi driver benefits from distributing the QR code because it creates future orders

### 9.13 Google Maps Presence

Create a Google Business Profile or map listings if the model allows it.

Examples:

- Russian Tourist Help Hoi An
- Hoi An Russian Guide
- Russian Assistant in Hoi An

Also collect reviews from the first customers. For tourists, Google Maps often becomes a decision point once they are already on site.

### 9.14 Spa and Massage Partnerships

Spa is one of the most natural monetization channels for tourists.

Ideas:

- Russian-language treatment menu
- QR code: "Find a good massage"
- Booking commission
- Rating of verified salons
- "Non-tourist price"
- Matching by request: relaxing, strong, foot, couple, clean, luxury

### 9.15 Currency Exchange and Crypto Exchange Partnerships

For Russian tourists, this can be a very important pain point due to card problems.

Idea:

- "Where to exchange money, USDT, or rubles"
- Verified exchange points
- Instructions in Russian
- Commission from the exchange point
- QR code in exchange locations
- Warning about risks and poor rates

### 9.16 Motorbike Rental Partnerships

Many tourists look for motorcycle and scooter rentals.

Idea:

- Verified bikes
- Help in Russian
- Explanation of the rules
- Photo and video of the vehicle condition
- Insurance, deposit, and what to do in case of an accident
- Rental commission
- QR code at rental shops

### 9.17 Airport Activation on Arrival Days of Relevant Flights

Send an assistant to Da Nang Airport on the days and hours when flights from the target countries arrive: Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Belarus, and other CIS directions.

Tasks for the assistant on site:

- Show service advertising to Russian-speaking tourists
- Offer a free mini-guide, QR code, or welcome bonus
- Possibly distribute free SIM cards or help with activation
- Observe who meets the tourists and which companies handle transfers
- Approach bus drivers, coordinators, and guides
- Collect contacts of transfer companies
- Try to negotiate advertising in buses, flyers, QR codes, or a commission per lead

### 9.18 Free Electric Bike Charging at Restaurants and Venues

Approach restaurants, cafes, and venues that have free space in front of the entrance and offer them the opportunity to become a free electric bike charging point for our customers.

Core concept:

- The venue places a normal extension cord or power outlet outside
- We place a small sign or stand with a QR code nearby
- The tourist scans the QR code and enters our Telegram, WhatsApp, or communication channel
- Free charging is activated or confirmed through us
- The venue receives a small payment for each charging session
- While the bike is charging, the tourist is likely to go inside and buy coffee, food, or drinks

Benefits for the venue:

- Additional foot traffic
- Potential tourist orders
- Payment for each charging session
- The venue looks more convenient for tourists
- Almost no cost if the outlet already exists

Benefits for us:

- We get the tourist's messenger contact
- We create physical points of presence in the city
- We offer a useful free service
- We can later sell transfers, excursions, restaurants, SIM cards, exchange, laundry, bikes, and other services

### 9.19 Russian Menu for Restaurants Through QR Code

Approach restaurants and offer to translate their menu into Russian for free or almost free.

Core concept:

- The restaurant gives us its menu
- We translate it into Russian
- We create an online version of the menu
- We place a QR code in the restaurant: "Menu in Russian"
- The Russian-speaking tourist scans the QR code
- The menu opens through our channel, page, mini-app, or messenger flow
- The user remains inside our system, and we can continue communicating with them

Benefits for the restaurant:

- Russian tourists can order more easily
- Higher chance that the tourist does not leave because the menu is unclear
- Higher average check
- The restaurant gets a ready-made Russian menu without extra work
- Russian phrases can be added for waiters

Benefits for us:

- We receive inbound traffic from already warm tourists
- We understand which restaurants generate more leads
- We can offer the tourist other services
- In the future we can charge restaurants for translation, menu updates, or promotion
- We create a network of partner venues

### 9.20 Restaurant Payment Assistance for Russian Tourists

Offer restaurants a service for situations where a Russian tourist cannot pay with their card or with a QR code.

Core concept:

- The restaurant places our QR code or contact point near the payment area
- If the tourist cannot complete the payment, they message us
- We help resolve the payment problem
- This can become both a useful emergency service for the tourist and a trust-building service for the restaurant

Value for the restaurant:

- Lower risk of losing the sale because of payment issues
- Better experience for Russian-speaking tourists
- A practical reason to cooperate with us even before larger partnership deals

Value for us:

- We capture the tourist contact at a high-intent moment
- We create another entry point into the service
- We can later offer additional services to the tourist
