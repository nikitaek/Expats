# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 1. Executive Summary

### What is the project

Travel AIgency is an AI travel agency for Russian-speaking tourists in Asia, available in messengers.

### What problem is being solved

The project addresses the needs of tourists who face language barriers, information chaos, fear of being deceived, payment difficulties, and a lack of trusted local help.

### What solution is offered

Travel AIgency provides an AI concierge in messengers that helps tourists find suitable providers, checks providers, and recommends providers.

### Why now

The opportunity is driven by the growth of Russian tourism to Vietnam, new direct flights, a limited number of accessible destinations for Russian travelers, and the growth of package tourism.

### Why AI provides an advantage

AI enables automation, support at scale, multilingual operations, and efficient service through messengers.
