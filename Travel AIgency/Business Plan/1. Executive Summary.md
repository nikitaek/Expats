# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 1. Executive Summary

### What is the project

Travel AIgency is an AI travel agency for Russian-speaking tourists in Asia, available in messengers, where monetization comes from concierge services provided via subcontractors who share revenues.

### What problem is being solved

Tourists, and especially Russian tourists, often do not know English, which makes communication very difficult. They also face information chaos, fear of being deceived, payment difficulties, and a lack of trusted local help.

### What solution is offered

Travel AIgency provides an AI concierge in messengers that helps tourists with all their travel needs: booking tours, finding a private driver, finding a restaurant or spa with a Russian menu, renting a bike, changing hotels, updating flights, ordering food, fruit, or alcohol delivery, exchanging money, and helping them pay when they have payment problems.

### Why now

The opportunity is driven by the growth of Russian tourism to Vietnam, new direct flights, the limited number of accessible destinations for Russian travelers, the growth of package tourism, and the increase in flights to Vietnam starting on May 23.
