### Monetization After the Trip

- Selling the next trip
- Partnerships with tour operators
- Insurance sales
- Re-engagement for the next destination in Vietnam or Southeast Asia
- Follow-up upsells based on the tourist's previous behavior and preferences
- White-label or partner offers sent after the trip through the same messenger channel
