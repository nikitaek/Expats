### Guides That Help Customers Adapt Better During the Trip

- City adaptation guides
- Local behavior and etiquette guides
- Transport and taxi guides
- Restaurant and food ordering guides
- Pharmacy and medicine guides
- Currency exchange and payment guides
- SIM card and internet setup guides
- Safety and scam avoidance guides

### Monetization After the Trip

- Selling the next trip
- Partnerships with tour operators
- Insurance sales
- Re-engagement for the next destination in Vietnam or Southeast Asia
- Follow-up upsells based on the tourist's previous behavior and preferences
- White-label or partner offers sent after the trip through the same messenger channel
