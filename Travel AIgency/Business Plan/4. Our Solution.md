# Travel AIgency Business Plan

## 4. Our Solution

This is a core section of the business plan.

### AI concierge in messengers

The client writes in:

- Telegram
- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- MAX
- Facebook Messenger

And receives help for real travel tasks, not only information.

### What we do

At the beginning, we do not provide services ourselves.

We:

- Find providers
- Check providers
- Recommend providers
- Communicate with providers
- Route customer requests to the right subcontractor or partner
- Manage the conversation between the tourist and the local provider
- Help complete the service, not only recommend it

Travel AIgency sits between the tourist and the local business as a trust and operations layer.

For example, if a tourist orders fruit, the system knows the tourist's location, identifies which partner should handle the delivery, and sends the request to that contractor. The tourist communicates with us, and we coordinate the execution with the partner.

The same logic applies to payments and other service requests: the tourist communicates in our chat, and we forward or route the required communication to the partner side.

This is not only an information service. Travel AIgency actively helps make the service happen.

### Future direct service delivery

Over time, once we understand which services have the highest margins, we may provide some of them ourselves.

One likely example is bike rental. The investment can be relatively low, and if demand is strong, we could increase profits by owning bike inventory in the most popular locations.

### Haindler as the operating system

Haindler connects to many messenger applications and manages conversations across them.

It serves as:

- Unified inbox
- Automation
- AI
- CRM
- Handover to a human when needed

Because Haindler knows key customer information in the CRM, it can route requests to the right subcontractors and partner companies that will perform the service.

In rare cases, the conversation is handed over to a human. In general, however, the communication model is AI-driven and two-sided: the AI communicates with the customer and also helps coordinate communication with the partner network.
