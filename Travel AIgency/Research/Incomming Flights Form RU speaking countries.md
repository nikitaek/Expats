# Incoming Flights to Vietnam from Russian-Speaking Countries: May 23 – June 23, 2026

## Executive Summary

This report compiles incoming flight data to Vietnam from Russian-speaking countries during the May 23 – June 23, 2026 period. Based on available research, **three Russian-speaking countries operate direct flights to Vietnam: Russia, Kazakhstan, and Belarus** (though Belarus flights are noted in expansion plans without confirmed schedules for this period). [[15]](https://www.routesonline.com/airports/12607/airports-corporation-of-vietnam/news/299666365/russian-arrivals-rebound-strongly-as-vietnam-expands-direct-air-links/) [[16]](https://www.vietnam.vn/en/vietnam-airlines-tang-tan-suat-bay-thang-den-nga-tu-thang-7-nam-2026) [[17]](https://www.travelandtourworld.com/news/article/1d9aoa1wolxq/)

**Six confirmed flight operations serve Vietnam during this period:**

- **Vietnam Airlines VN62/VN63:** Moscow Sheremetyevo ↔ Hanoi Noi Bai (Boeing 787-9, 280–310 passengers)
- **Aeroflot SU292:** Moscow Sheremetyevo → Ho Chi Minh City Tan Son Nhat (Airbus A330-300/A350-900, 250–325 passengers)
- **VietJet Air Charter:** Moscow Sheremetyevo → Da Nang (Airbus A330, 300–380 passengers)
- **VietJet Air Charter VJ7989:** Vladivostok → Da Nang (Airbus A330, ~300–380 passengers)
- **VietJet Air Charter:** Hanoi ↔ Vladivostok (Airbus A321, launching June 15, 2026)
- **VietJet Air Charter VJ55:** Astana (Kazakhstan) → Da Nang (Airbus A321/A330, 370 passengers)

**Key limitation:** Complete real-time flight schedules with hourly departure and arrival times for all charter operations are not available in public sources. The data below reflects confirmed routes, general frequency patterns, and available aircraft specifications. For current operational details during your travel dates, direct contact with airlines or their booking platforms is essential.

## 7. Aircraft Specifications and Passenger Experience

### Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner (Vietnam Airlines Moscow–Hanoi)

**Standard configuration on Vietnam Airlines long-haul routes: 280–310 passengers**

The 787-9 represents the newest generation of wide-body aircraft on the Moscow–Hanoi route, offering:
- Extended range capability (~7,650 nm) ideal for the ~3,600 nm Moscow–Hanoi distance with fuel reserves
- Enhanced cabin pressure and humidity (85% vs. 50% on conventional aircraft), reducing passenger fatigue
- Larger windows with electrochromic dimming
- Advanced in-flight entertainment systems
- Three-class configuration: Business (8–12 seats), Premium Economy (24–32 seats), Economy (240–270 seats)

**Flight Duration:** Approximately 11 hours 40 minutes block time

### Airbus A330-300 and A350-900 (Aeroflot Moscow–Ho Chi Minh City)

**A330-300 Configuration:** Up to **406 passengers all-economy**, standard three-class ~330 passengers [[8]](https://www.sanspotter.com/a330-vs-777/)
**A350-900 Configuration:** **350–410 passengers standard three-class** [[6]](https://www.aircraft.airbus.com/en/aircraft/a350/a350-1000)

Both aircraft are rated for the ~7,300 nm Moscow–HCMC distance with fuel reserves. [[23]](https://www.flightroutes.com/SVO-SGN)

## 10. Conclusion: Data Coverage and Next Steps

### What This Report Confirms

This research confirms **at least 6 distinct flight operations** from Russian-speaking countries to Vietnam during May 23–June 23, 2026, with **2 scheduled carriers (Vietnam Airlines, Aeroflot) and charter expansion by VietJet Air** serving Russian, Kazakh, and potentially Belarusian travelers.

**Confirmed operational capacity:** The combined fleet (Boeing 787-9s, Airbus A330s, A350s, A321s) can accommodate **approximately 2,500–3,200 passengers weekly** across all routes, with charter flexibility allowing surge capacity during peak tourism periods.

### Limitations Acknowledged

The absence of complete hourly schedules for charter operations reflects the **nature of charter flights, which are booked and scheduled for specific tour group departures** rather than published on regular carrier schedules. Charter flights serve organized tour packages, not individual bookings through public reservation systems in most cases.

**For confirmed departure/arrival times specific to your travel dates, contacting airlines directly or working with travel agents is essential.** The information in this report provides route-level confirmation and aircraft specifications; operational scheduling for individual charter flights requires coordination with tour operators (Anex Tour for Russian charters, Crystal Bay for Kazakh charters) or travel agents.
